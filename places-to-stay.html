Places to stay

combine comfort with your travels

hotels

hotel ripa trastavere

Lounge in the heart of Rome in the rooms and suites of this hotel. It is conveniently connected by bus, train, and tram stations and the guests' favourite feature is the Ripa Place restaurant.

4★ | ££

the pantheon iconic rome hotel pantheon

The 79 rooms and suites at this hotel fuse elegance and contemporary design. Offers such as a panoramic terrace and views of the pantheon are combined with a local cuisine.

5★ | £££

relais le clarisse trastavere

Enjoy a quaint and green stay in the centre of the town of Trastevere, walking distance from the most notable sights in Rome. Each room contains am intimate charm for your restful haven.

3★ | ££

palazzo cardinal cesi vatican city

This palace holds 29 cherry wood decorated rooms with a courtyard, luxe library, and old refectory. This hotel is moments away from St. Peters Square with an elegant atmosphere.

4★ | £££

hotel dei mellini prati

This 19th century hotel hosts a private art collection from local artists, an outdoor patio, and pets can stay free. Relax by the sights of River Tiber in one of the 80 rooms and 2 meeting rooms for up to 70 people.

4★ | £££

eitch borromini palazzo pamphilj

Originally a baroque church, enrich your stay with Roman history. Enjoy breakfast on the terrace and views of the Fountain of the Four River.

5★ | £££
