the most romantic neighbourhood, Aventine Hill.

Aventine hill is the southernmost of Rome's seven hills. It is a serene and residential area away from Roman noise. Whilst displaying an evocative setting it is perfect for a romantic getaway and a peaceful break.

the most authentic, Monti District.

The Monti District or Rione Monti as it is called in Italian is a neighbourhood located in the city center. Monti charms its visitors with small alleys, significant monuments, and independant stores. Take a stroll down the cobbled streets for a true taste of Italy.

best for sightseeing, Ancient Rome.

Rome's ancient heart takes the spotlight with its iconic landmarks. The Colosseum, Pantheon, Piazza Navona and countless others are all located here. Each glimpse of breathtaking history is truly a sight for sore eyes.

best for dining, Testaccio.

This dynamic stop in Rome elevates the classic Italian cuisine to new heights. Whether its humble street food or chic restaurants, the taste of Testaccio is the perfect host.
